Cinque Terre

Cinque Terre, translated as Five Lands in English, is a group of five picturesque coastal towns located in the region of Liguria, Italy, which has been described as one of the most beautiful places in the world. This is entirely justified because it really is like heaven on earth. Here are some interesting facts about this wonder that you may not have known. The five towns of Cinque Terre are Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore. The towns of Cinque Terre are known for their colourful houses and buildings, which are built into the cliffs along the coast.

Cinque Terre is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, recognized for its cultural and natural significance. The area is also a national park and marine protected area. Cinque Terre is a popular destination for hiking, with several scenic trails connecting the five towns.
